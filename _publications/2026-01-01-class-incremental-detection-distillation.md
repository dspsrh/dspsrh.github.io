Ongoing independent research on catastrophic forgetting in class-incremental object detection. The study reproduces and extends continual-learning baselines (LwF, iCaRL, ERD, YOLO-IOD) and evaluates teacher-student distillation under standard incremental-detection protocols on COCO.
