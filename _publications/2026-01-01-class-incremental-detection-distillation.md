Ongoing independent research on class-incremental object detection. The project reproduces strong KD-based incremental detection baselines, especially YOLO-IOD and ERD, fine-tunes YOLO-World on COCO with alternative backbone variants, and investigates a smoother KD loss to reduce gradient explosion from noisy teacher responses and false pseudo-labels.
